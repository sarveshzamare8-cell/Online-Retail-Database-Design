Tasks Performed:

Identified core entities:

Customers

Products

Orders

Order_Items

Payments

Defined attributes for each entity:

Customers: customer_id, name, email, phone, address

Products: product_id, product_name, category, price, stock_quantity

Orders: order_id, customer_id, order_date, order_status, total_amount

Order_Items: order_item_id, order_id, product_id, quantity, unit_price

Payments: payment_id, order_id, payment_date, payment_method, payment_status

Established relationships:

One customer can place multiple orders

One order can contain multiple products (via Order_Items)

Each order has one payment record

Defined primary keys and foreign keys for all entities.

Created ER diagram using dbdiagram.io to visually represent entities, relationships, and constraints.
